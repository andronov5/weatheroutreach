Meet Lis Cohen

A weather and climate communicator who makes complex science feel understandable.

Through Weather Outreach, Lis combines meteorology, climate adaptation, public policy, visual storytelling, and a warm teaching style to help audiences connect with the atmosphere around them.

Her background spans local government climate resilience, atmospheric science training, university instruction, national parks education, science communication, public engagement, and weather photography.
