About Lis Cohen

About Lis

Lis Cohen, MS, MPA, is a meteorologist, climate adaptation leader, educator, photographer, and science communicator.

Lis Cohen, MS, MPA

Founder of Weather Outreach. Meteorologist, educator, climate adaptation strategist, speaker, and photographer.

Her Story

Weather, climate, and community.

Lis has spent her career helping people understand the atmosphere and prepare for a changing climate.

Her work has moved through classrooms, museums, national parks, universities, public policy, local government, media, and community events.

Weather Outreach brings that background together in one place: practical science, warm communication, and a clear connection to everyday life.
