About Lis Cohen

Meteorologist. Climate resilience leader. Public educator. Science communicator.

Lis Cohen, MS, MPA, brings together atmospheric science, policy, community engagement, and education to help people understand weather and prepare for a changing climate.

Lis Cohen, MS, MPA

Founder of Weather Outreach. Meteorologist, educator, photographer, climate adaptation strategist, and public speaker.

Her Story

A career built at the intersection of weather, people, policy, and place.

Lis has worked across climate adaptation, atmospheric science education, public policy, national parks, museums, universities, media, and community engagement.

Her work is especially strong where science needs to become practical: helping people understand risk, prepare for extreme weather, build resilience, and see the atmosphere as something that affects daily life.

Weather Outreach should market that range clearly: Lis is not just someone who knows weather. She can teach it, communicate it, photograph it, connect it to climate resilience, and make it meaningful for different audiences.
